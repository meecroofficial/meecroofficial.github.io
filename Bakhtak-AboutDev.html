My Journey

Hi, my name is Hassan Ali, I'm 18 years old, and this is my very first horror game. Ever since I was younger, I've loved horror games, and one of my biggest inspirations was Granny. That game sparked the idea in me to try creating my own horror experience, and after a lot of learning, I finally made it happen.

I live in Swat, Pakistan, and I recently completed my secondary school studies in Computer Science. My next goal is to continue my education in Cyber Security at university/college, because I'm passionate about protecting people and systems in the digital world.

Apart from this game, I have also developed a few mobile apps, but this project is special to me since it marks my first real step into game development. I see it as the beginning of a journey where I can mix my creativity with my technical skills, while still working towards my dream of becoming a Cyber Security expert.
