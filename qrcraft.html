Privacy Policy for QRCraft

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how we handle your information in our QR code scanning and generation app.

Last Updated: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how QRCraft collects, uses, and protects your information. By using QRCraft, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

Required solely for scanning QR codes. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

Generated QR Codes

Data you input to generate QR codes (e.g., URLs, text) is processed locally and never uploaded to our servers.

Scan History

Saved locally on your device (optional) and never shared externally.

AdMob Data

Google AdMob may collect anonymized data (device ID, IP address) for personalized ads, as per Google's policies.

How We Use Information

QR Functionality

Camera access is used in real-time for scanning; generated QR data remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

Advertising

AdMob serves non-intrusive ads (banner and interstitial) using standard ad-serving technologies.

We use carefully placed ads to support development while maintaining a positive user experience.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Follows Google's Privacy Policy. We only use AdMob for ad serving in our free version.

View Policy

ZXing Library

Open-source QR scanning library. Does not collect any user data.

Data Security

All user-generated data (scan history, QR codes) is stored locally and can be cleared via the app's "Clear History" feature.

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties beyond what's necessary for ad serving through Google AdMob.

Your generated QR codes and scan history never leave your device and are not accessible by us or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

QRCraft complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Policy Updates

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app signifies your acceptance of any changes.

We will notify users of significant changes through app updates or in-app notifications.
