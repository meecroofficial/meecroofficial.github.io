Privacy Policy

Data Collection

QRCraft is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera Access - Required for scanning QR codes and barcodes. We never store images or video from your camera.
  • Photo Library Access - Optional permission to scan QR codes from your existing photos.

All processing happens locally on your device. No data ever leaves your phone.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data generated within the app:

  • All generated QR codes are stored only on your device
  • Scan history is encrypted and never synced to cloud services
  • No third-party analytics or tracking frameworks
  • No advertisements or data monetization

When you delete history items, they are immediately and permanently removed.

Data Sharing

QRCraft does not share any data with third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • When you explicitly choose to share a generated QR code
  • When you export your scan history (you control what is exported)

We do not use any third-party SDKs that might collect data without your knowledge.

Your Rights

As a QRCraft user, you have full control over your data:

  • Access all data stored by the app through the History section
  • Delete individual items or clear entire history at any time
  • Export your data in CSV format for your records
  • Completely disable history storage in Settings

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no need for data subject requests.
