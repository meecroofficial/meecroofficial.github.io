About Meecro

The story behind SEOTuber and the developer who created it

What is Meecro?

Meecro isn't a big company or a team of developers. It's just me - Hassan Ali - a passionate developer who loves creating tools that solve real problems.

The name "Meecro" represents my approach to development: creating micro-tools that make macro differences in people's daily workflows. Each tool I build aims to simplify a specific task that might otherwise be complicated or time-consuming.

After completing my secondary education, I decided to focus on building practical applications that help content creators, starting with SEOTuber for YouTube optimization. My goal is to create tools that are powerful yet simple to use, without the complexity of enterprise software.
