Horror Game Privacy Policy

⚠️ 17+ AGE RESTRICTION
This horror game contains content suitable only for ages 17 and older

Data Collection

App data: The game is single-player with no login, accounts, or analytics, so it does not directly collect personal user data (name, email, etc.). No location or device data (beyond what the ad SDK collects) is tracked by the game itself.

AdMob data: The only data collection comes via Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically collects device information (IP address, Android Advertising ID, App Set ID, etc.), basic user interaction data (app launches, taps, video views), and diagnostic info for ad serving and analytics (developers.google.com).

Firebase: Although Firebase Auth is included in the project, it is not used, so no authentication or personal data is collected or stored from users via Firebase.

Data Usage

In-app use: The game does not use any user data beyond displaying ads. No user behavior or game-play analytics are collected or used.

Ad data usage: AdMob uses the collected identifiers and device info to deliver and target ads. This data is used by Google's ad network only, for personalized ad purposes and preventing fraud. The game developer does not access or use this data for any other purpose.

Personal Information

No personal info stored: Since there is no user registration or profile, the app does not collect or store personal identifiers (names, emails, contacts, etc.). No personal health or sensitive information is involved.

Data retention: Because no personal data is collected, nothing is stored on our servers. All ad-related data remains with Google AdMob per their policies.

Third-Party Sharing

Google AdMob: Data collected by the app (via the AdMob SDK) is shared only with Google for advertising purposes (developers.google.com). We must disclose that Google (AdMob/AdSense) may receive device IDs, IP addresses, and other info to serve ads.

Other third parties: We do not share any data with other third-party services or ad networks beyond Google AdMob. No analytics or social SDKs are integrated.

Children's Privacy & Age Restrictions

Not for children: This horror game is not intended for children under 13 due to its scary/disturbing content. In line with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), we state that we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (support.google.com).

By using this application, you confirm that you are 17 years of age or older and accept the terms of this privacy policy.
