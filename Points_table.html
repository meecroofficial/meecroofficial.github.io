Tournament Rules & Regulations

Important Rules for All Participants

  • All players must use their registered PUBG Mobile accounts. No account sharing is allowed.
  • Matches will be played in TPP Squad mode on the Erangel map.
  • Any form of cheating, hacking, or using third-party applications will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Teaming up with enemy squads is strictly prohibited. Players caught teaming will be banned from the tournament.
  • Players must join the match lobby within 10 minutes of the scheduled time.
  • In case of a disconnect, the player must reconnect immediately. The match will not be restarted.
  • Players must maintain sportsmanlike conduct. Toxic behavior may lead to disqualification.
  • All decisions by tournament administrators are final and binding.
  • Prize money will be distributed via EasyPaisa/JazzCash within 3 working days after tournament completion.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Any violation of these rules will result in immediate disqualification of the entire team without refund of entry fee. Repeated offenses may lead to permanent ban from future tournaments.

Points System

Kill Points

Points are awarded for each enemy elimination

2
Points per kill

Each player kill earns 2 points for the team

Placement Points

Points based on final team position

The higher the placement, the more points awarded

Total Points Calculation

Total Points = (Kill Points × 2) + Placement Points

Example: A team with 10 kills finishing 3rd would get (10 × 2) + 10 = 30 points
