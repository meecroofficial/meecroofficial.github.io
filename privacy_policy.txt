Privacy Policy for Sharpify AI

Last Updated: 2025-12-12

Sharpify AI (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our image enhancement and upscaling tools.

1. Information We Collect
- The App does NOT collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.
- All image processing is performed locally on your device.
- No images, metadata, or device information leave your device.
- We do not create accounts, track users, or use analytics.

2. Image Processing
- Images selected for enhancement or upscaling remain entirely offline.
- We do not upload your images to any server or third‑party service.
- You retain full ownership of your images.

3. Advertising
- The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization as per their policies.
- We do not have access to or store any data collected by Google.
- You may opt out of personalized ads through Google’s device settings.

4. Children’s Privacy
- Our App is not directed to children under 13.
- We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

5. Policy Updates
- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
- Updates will be reflected in this document with a new “Last Updated” date.
